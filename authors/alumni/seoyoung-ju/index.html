Seoyoung Ju

IMSI Lab Alumni

Korea University

Seoyoung Ju is an IMSI Lab alumnus who is currently pursuing an Integrated M.S.-Ph.D. in Artificial Intelligence at Korea University. During her time at IMSI Lab, she made significant contributions to medical imaging and AI research. She graduated with distinction from Sangmyung University with a B.E. in Human-Centered Artificial Intelligence, achieving excellent academic performance (Overall GPA: 4.2/4.5, Major GPA: 4.36/4.5).

Her research focuses on medical AI, computer vision, and deep learning applications. She has been actively involved in cutting-edge research projects and has co-authored multiple papers in prestigious conferences including ISBI, ICEIC, and IEEE APCCAS. She is also an active mentor and executive at OUTTA, contributing to the AI research community.
